We are

Goodpack

We are a global supply chain solution provider. We provide resilient, cost-efficient, and sustainable pay-per-use supply chain solutions through the largest fleet of reusable pallet-sized intermediate bulk containers (IBCs).

CREATING CIRCULAR SUPPLY CHAINS

Goodpack’s pay-per-use container services enable supply chain solutions that can be seamlessly integrated into the world’s largest distribution networks.

Optimizing and protecting payloads

Goodpack’s rugged, robust and reusable containers represent efficient, safe and environmentally sustainable packaging solutions that are delivered locally and collected globally.

Transforming logistics through technology

Goodpack’s global technology laboratory, The Forge, is an innovation hub that enables our engineering teams to work together with customers to develop the tools to meet the demands of today’s global supply chains.

Featured container solution - TYRECUBE®

A good example of Goodpack’s innovative thinking is TYRECUBE, a strategic alliance developed together with CEVA Logistics, a world-leading supply chain management company. TYRECUBE is a reusable container solution dedicated to optimizing tyre storage and transport.

Case Studies
